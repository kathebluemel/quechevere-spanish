  • Interpersonal: Students sustain a basic exchange, ask questions back, and use rejoinders to negotiate meaning.
  • Presentational: Students produce a short written and spoken self-introduction focused on comprehensibility.

Proficiency Target

  • Novice Low ‚Üí Novice Mid (formulaic language; emerging sentence creation; understood by sympathetic listener/reader).

  • I can exchange basic personal information using memorized and practiced phrases, asking at least one question back.
  • I can present a short self-introduction with understandable pronunciation and message clarity.
  • I can compare greeting norms and identify cultural diversity within the Spanish-speaking world.
