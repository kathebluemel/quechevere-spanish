Unit 7: Family & Relationships

Talk about family members, relationships, and basic descriptions using high-frequency phrases.

Proficiency: Novice Mid → Novice High (supported) High-frequency family words Ser + descriptions • Tener • Hay
Proficiency Goal

Students understand and communicate about family members, relationships, and basic descriptions in familiar contexts using practiced language.

Essential Question

¿Cómo influye la familia en quiénes somos?

Core Language Targets
  • I can name common family members (madre, padre, hermano/a, etc.).
  • I can describe a person using ser + adjectives.
  • I can say what someone has using tener.
  • I can ask and answer simple questions about family.
Unit 7 “Must-Have” Vocabulary
Family: madre, padre, hermano/a, abuelo/a, tío/a, primo/a, familia, amigos
Descriptions: alto/a, bajo/a, simpático/a, serio/a, trabajador/a, inteligente, cómico/a
Structures: Es… / Son… • Tiene… • Hay… • ¿Quién es…? • ¿Cómo es…?

Stage 1: Understand Interpretive • Comprehension first
▾

Learning Targets

  • I can understand the main idea of a short description of a family.
  • I can identify who is who (mother/father/sibling).
  • I can match adjectives to meaning.

Lectura (10+ sentences)

En mi casa hay cinco personas. Mi madre se llama Ana y es muy simpática. Mi padre se llama Carlos y es trabajador. Tengo un hermano y una hermana. Mi hermano es alto y cómico. Mi hermana es inteligente y un poco seria. Mis abuelos viven cerca y son muy amables. Los domingos, mi familia come juntos. A mi madre le gusta cocinar y a mi padre le gusta mirar deportes. Yo soy estudiante y tengo muchos amigos. Mi familia es importante para mí.

Comprehension Questions (in English)

  1. How many people are in the family?
  2. What is the mother’s name and what is she like?
  3. What is the father’s name and what is he like?
  4. What is the brother like?
  5. What is the sister like?
  6. What does the family do on Sundays?

Sentence Frames

  • En mi familia hay…
  • Mi ____ es ____.
  • Tengo ____.
  • Mi familia es importante porque…
Stage 3: Create Presentational • Describe your family
▾

Task

Write or record a short description (6–8 sentences) about your family. Include: who, how many people, and 3 descriptions.

Checklist

  • Uses hay or a number
  • Uses ser + adjective at least 3 times
  • Includes at least 2 family words
